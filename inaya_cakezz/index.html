About Us

Welcome to Inaya Cakezz

Established with passion and dedication, Inaya Cakezz is a premium cake shop located in Shivsagar, Sasaram. Founded by Tauqeer Kazmi, our bakery is known for creating delicious and visually stunning cakes for all occasions.

Our master baker, Neda Kazmi, brings over 7 years of experience in the art of cake making, ensuring that every creation is not just a treat for the taste buds but also a feast for the eyes.

At Inaya Cakezz, we take pride in using only the finest ingredients to craft our cakes. From traditional flavors to custom designs, our team pays meticulous attention to every detail to exceed your expectations.
